Frequently asked questions

How does the AI-ML de-smoking/de-hazing algorithm work?

The algorithm works by analyzing real-time video footage from areas affected by indoor fires. It uses machine learning techniques to identify and separate smoke and haze from the actual scene. Then, it enhances the video feed, removing the interference caused by smoke and haze, thus providing a clearer view for rescue teams.

Can this algorithm be used in any indoor fire situation?

Yes, the algorithm is designed to be versatile and can be applied to various indoor fire scenarios. It adapts to different levels of smoke and haze, making it effective in a wide range of indoor fire hazards.

Is the algorithm capable of working in real-time?

Absolutely, real-time processing is a key feature of this algorithm. It analyzes and enhances the video feed on the fly, ensuring that rescue teams have an up-to-date view of the situation as it evolves.
